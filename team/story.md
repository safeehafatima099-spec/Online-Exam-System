Online Exam Universe is a digital world where everything works like a Linked List.
The system starts from Node Prime, which triggers the beginning of the exam.
Pointer Nexus connects each question as the exam progresses.
List Core maintains all student data, records, and scores.
Each question is a node, and Pointer Nexus moves the flow forward.
Scores update instantly on correct answers.
Traversal completes at the final question and results are calculated.
This universe ensures a smooth, simple, and structured exam experience.
